# Wallpapers

The wallpapers aren't in the repo. They're full-res art from [wallhaven.cc](https://wallhaven.cc) that other people made, so shipping the actual files would just re-host someone else's work and add about 150 MB of binaries. Instead, here's every wallpaper I use with a link back to the source.

Each file on my machine is named after its wallhaven id (`wh-<id>`), so anything in the screenshots maps straight to a row below. Click a thumbnail to open the full page, grab the resolution you want, and drop it in `wallpapers/`.

Featured in the demo video: `jexkwm` (the main one) and `gwpgv3`.
